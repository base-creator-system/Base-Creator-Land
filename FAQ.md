# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1?

The BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 is a creator-owned software template used to build independent Creator Deployments.

It provides the tools, architecture, and workflows needed to publish and operate creator experiences.

---

## What is a Creator Deployment?

A Creator Deployment is an independent application built using the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1.

Each deployment represents an individual creator and remains under that creator's control.

---

## Who owns the Creator Deployment?

Creators own their own:

- Creator Identity
- Creator Branding
- Creator Content
- Creator Media
- Creator Communications
- Creator Audience Relationships

Ownership of a Creator Deployment does not transfer ownership of the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1.

---

## Is the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 open source?

No.

The BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 is proprietary software distributed under its own License.

---

## Can I customize my deployment?

Yes.

The system is designed to allow creators to customize their deployment while preserving the integrity of the underlying software.

---

## Can I host it anywhere?

Yes.

The BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 is designed to remain hosting independent.

Compatible hosting providers and private infrastructure may be used.

---

## Can I move my deployment to another hosting provider?

Yes.

Changing hosting providers does not change your Creator Deployment or the identity of the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1.

---

## Does the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 collect user data?

The software itself is designed to operate without requiring user accounts, audience tracking, or software-level data collection.

Creators may choose to integrate independent third-party services, which operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

---

## Does the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 guarantee future updates?

No.

Future releases, updates, maintenance, and support may be offered separately.

---
