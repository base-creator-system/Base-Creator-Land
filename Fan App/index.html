Install This App

Add this app to your phone so it opens like a regular app from your Home Screen.


iPhone / iPad
  1. Open this app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.
▶
Android
  1. Open this app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu.
  3. Tap Install App or Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Install.
Once installed, the app icon will appear on your device and open directly from your Home Screen.
