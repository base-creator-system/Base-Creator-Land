# BASE Rights and Ownership

## Purpose

This document defines the rights and ownership boundaries between the **BASE Creator System**, the **BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1**, and individual Creator Deployments.

---

## Definitions

**BASE Creator System** means the overall creator system, including its architecture, workflows, design principles, and long-term evolution.

**BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1** means the licensed software product, including its software, Creator Dashboard, Fan App, documentation, publishing workflow, and deployment workflow.

**BASE Brand** means the BASE name, BASE logo, BASE identity, BASE marks, and associated branding.

**Creator Deployment** means a specific implementation built using the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 for a creator, artist, business, organization, project, or brand.

**Creator Identity** means the creator's name, stage name, logo, visual identity, business identity, and public-facing presentation.

**Creator Content** means creator-supplied photos, videos, audio, music, text, artwork, links, live information, community links, supporter information, and media.

**Creator Audience** means the creator's direct relationships, supporters, customers, fans, email contacts, community relationships, and audience connections.

Each creator retains ownership of their own:

- Creator Identity

- Creator Audience

- Creator Logos

- Creator Public Presentation

---

# Separation

The BASE Creator System provides the software.

Creators provide their own identity, branding, content, media, and audience.

Using the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 does not transfer creator rights to William Smith McClinton.

Using the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 does not transfer ownership of the BASE Creator System or the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1 to the creator.

---

# Future Evolution

The BASE Creator System may continue to evolve through future releases, licensing models, governance, commercial offerings, and written agreements.

Nothing contained within this document transfers ownership or intellectual property unless expressly stated in a separate written legal agreement.

---

The BASE Creator System is intended to preserve long-term ownership, creative control, and future opportunity for William Smith McClinton, his successors, family, and authorized assigns.

No informal use, pilot, deployment, setup, demonstration, or evaluation shall be interpreted as transferring ownership of the BASE Creator System, the BASE Tier 2 Creator Template v1, or the BASE Brand.
